Reacting Masses

Prerequisites

  1. Periodic table
  2. Formulae
  3. The mole
  4. Relative Formula Mass
  5. Formula Equations

Steps to follow

  1. Write a word equation
  2. Write a balanced formula equation
  3. calculate the Relative Formula Mass of the reactants and products
  4. multiply each RFM by the number of that molecule used by the balanced equation (ie by the big number in front of it)
  5. write g after each relative mass to convert them to masses
  6. work out how to convert the number you have for the molecule to the amount of it stated in the question
  7. Do the same conversion to each of the other relative masses
  8. Select the amount asked for in the question to report
