Halogen Displacement Answers

  1. F2 + 2NaBr --> 2NaF + Br2
  2. Clear potassium iodide solution would turn brown, firstly because of the Bromine added, but then because of displaced iodine in solution
  3. Br2 + 2KI --> 2NaBr + I2
  4. The chlorine displaces all the bromine from the sodium and magnesium bromide that is in the seawater. Seawater is a mixture of salts in aqueous solution.
    Cl2(g) + 2NaBr(aq) --> 2NaCl(aq) + Br2(l)
