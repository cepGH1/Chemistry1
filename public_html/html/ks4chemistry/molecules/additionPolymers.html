Addition Polymers

Polymers made by adding lots of monomers, each with a carbon carbon double bond in it C=C

Examples

poly(propene)

poly(chloroethene)PVC

poly(tetrafluoroethene)PTFE

polystyrene

General Formula

-[-CR1R2 - CR3R4-]n-

Each of the things attached to each of the carbon atoms at either end of the double bond, can be different. The repeated backbone of the polymer is a 2 carbon unit with a single covalent bond between the carbon atoms.

Organic chemists use R to represent any organic (carbon based) part of a molecule. Used if the formula/structure of that part of the molecule isn't important/involved in the reaction being shown.
