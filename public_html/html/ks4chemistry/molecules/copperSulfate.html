Copper Sulphate

Copper II Sulfate is a bright blue ionic salt that dissolves in water to form blue solutions.
Copper Sulfate contains Cu2+ ions and (SO4)2- ions

Copper Sulfate can be made from dilute sulfuric acid and copper oxide. This is an example of a neutralisation reaction.

Sulfuric Acid + Copper Oxide → Copper Sulfate + water
H2SO4(aq) + CuO → CuSO4(aq) + H2O(l)
