Litmus

  • Litmus has been used to test for acids for centuries.
  • Originally a mixture of compounds extracted from lichens.
  • The term 'litmus test' has become used in english to describe a critical or decisive test or question in any walk of life.
    For example; 'The litmus test for the track was whether the drop was memorable. This would decide if it became a hit'

Red in acid - Blue in alkali
