Atomic Structure

Modern ideas about the structure of the atom
The atom is made up of a very small, very dense, nucleus containing protons and neutrons. Outside the nucleus are the electrons, arranged into areas called shells or orbitals. These are places where there is a certain chance of finding the electrons.
The nucleus isn't drawn to scale in this image. It would be absolutely tiny.
