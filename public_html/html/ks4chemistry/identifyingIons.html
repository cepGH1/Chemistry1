Calcium Ca2+ 1. Sodium hydroxide solution is added followed by more (excess) sodium hydroxide solution
2. Ammonia solution is added followed by more (excess) ammonia solution
3. Flame test 		1. Forms a white precipitate that will not redissolve no matter how much sodium hydroxide is added.
2. No precipitate formed when ammonia solution added.
3. Red coloured flame test
Iron II Fe2+ Addition of dilute sodium hydroxide solution A green precipitate of iron II hydroxide is produced.
This precipitate will slowly oxidise to a red-brown precipitate of iron III hydroxide.
Iron III Fe3+ Addition of dilute sodium hydroxide solution A red-brown (rust coloured) precipitate of iron III hydroxide is produced
