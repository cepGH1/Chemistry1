Nitric Acid HNO3

A strong acid.
pH of 1 or less
Reacts with bases to create nitrate salts.

nitric acid + sodium hydroxide → sodium nitrate + water
HNO3(aq) + NaOH(s) → NaNO3(aq) + H2O(l)

nitric acid + potassium hydroxide → potassium nitrate + water
HNO3(aq) + KOH(s) → KNO3(aq) + H2O(l)
