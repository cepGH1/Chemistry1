Organic Chemistry (Carbon Compounds)

Life on Earth is made from carbon compounds. Many other useful materials,for example, drugs and plastics, are also compounds of carbon.
Carbon is capable of forming millions of different, covalently bonded, molecules that can be used for a huge variety of purposes.
