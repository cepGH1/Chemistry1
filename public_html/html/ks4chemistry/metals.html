Metals

Properties

In general many metals share the following physical properties
  • Hard
  • Shiny
  • Not brittle
  • Ductile (can be stretched without breaking)
  • Malleable (can be bent without breaking)
  • Have high melting points (with a small number of exceptions such as mercury)
  • They can be mixed together to make alloys that have different and often useful physical properties
Metals also have many chemical properties in common
  • React to form compounds that contain positive metal ions (cations)
  • Compounds are generally solids
  • Form basic hydroxides (a few make amphoteric oxides)
  • Form basic oxides
