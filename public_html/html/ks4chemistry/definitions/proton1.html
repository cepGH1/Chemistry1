Hydrogen Atom
A Hydrogen atom has 1 proton in it's nucleus
Hydrogen contains no neutrons!
Atomic number = 1 (because it has 1 proton)
Mass number = 1 (because number of protons + number of neutrons =1)
Removing the single electron from the hydrogen atom produces a hydrogen ion H+, made up of just a single proton

The name proton is often used for hydrogen cations
