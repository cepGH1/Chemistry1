Homologous Series

Compounds that are related by their formula and properties.
Members of an homologous series have the same functional groups
The can be represented by a general formula

Examples

Example 1 Alkanes

Methane CH4, Ethane C2H6, Propane C3H8
The number of the hydrogens is related to the number of carbons.
If you have n number of carbon atoms then in an alkane there will be (2n + 2) hydrogen atoms. Alkanes are an homologous series with a general formula CnH2n+2
