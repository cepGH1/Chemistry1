Acids

Any substance that dissociates to provide a proton(H+) in solution.

If the first letter of the formula is H, the compound may be an acid.

Acids that you need to know at GCSE
  • HCl Hydrochloric Acid
  • HNO3 Nitric Acid
  • H2SO4 Sulphuric Acid
  • CH3CH2COOH Ethanoic Acid

Turns universal indicator orange or red
