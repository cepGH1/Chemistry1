Purification

A substance is pure if it contains a single type of chemical
Purity is often reported as a percentage
Getting chemicals pure enough to analyse, identify and use is a big and important task for chemists. There are lots of ways to do this.
Here are some

Distillation

Boiling a mixture, then condensing and collecting a single pure product.

Fractional Distillation

Boiling a mixture,separating using a fractionating column and then condensing and collecting pure products one at a time.

Chromatography

Separating mixtures of compounds by passing them through some other substance.

Filtration

Separating by dissolving and filtering out solid impurities that fail to dissolve

Crystallisation

Making pure crystals from a concentrated impure solution
