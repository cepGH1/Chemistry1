The Chlor-Alkali Industry

Making industrially useful chemicals using sodium chloride as a starting material.
Chlorine, Sodium Hydroxide, and Hydrogen, are the three products.

Uses of chlorine

Chlorine gas is used to make other organic molecules that contain chlorine. These molecules can then be used in other reactions to make other compounds.
