Alkanes CnH2n+2

Alkanes are extacted from Crude Oil. Crude Oil is mostly a mixture of alkanes.
Alkanes contain only Carbon and Hydrogen atoms and they are bonded using only single covalent bonds.
The compounds form a series, called an homologous series. The formulae all have the structure CnH2n+2 where n is the number of Carbon atoms in the molecule
