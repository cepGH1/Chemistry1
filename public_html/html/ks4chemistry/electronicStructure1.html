Electronic Structure

Electrons are arranged in a series of energy shells.
Fill them from lowest to highest, until the electrons are all used.
Shells can hold
Shell
  • 1, up to 2 electrons
  • 2, up to 8 electrons
  • 3, up to 8 electrons
  • 4 - you will only have to put 1 or 2 electrons in this shell
