Reduction using Carbon (Blast Furnace Example)

Metals below Carbon in the reactivity series can be displaced from their salts using carbon. The metal ores are commonly oxides. Metal and Carbon Dioxide are the products of the reaction.

Examples

Zinc, Iron, Copper

Reactions

The reducing agent is Carbon Monoxide produced by burning coke in a limited supply of Oxygen

2C + O2 → 2CO

The Carbon Monoxide then reacts with Iron Oxide

Fe2O3 + 3CO → 2Fe + 3CO2
