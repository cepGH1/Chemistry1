Ion Charges

This page is designed to help learn about the charges on common simple ions
Ions loose or gain electrons in order to acquire a full outer shell. Revise electron structure of atoms and ions here
How to work out the likely electron structure and charge on an ion
  • If the outer shell is full the element is unlikely to form ions
  • If outer shell not full, add or remove electrons until a full shell is achieved
  • Do whichever takes least electrons
  • If you added electrons you made a negative ion
  • If you removed electrons you made a positive ion
