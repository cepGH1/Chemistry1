Answer 1

  1. Convert the time to minutes and then to seconds
    60 minutes in an hour
    3.5 hours = 3.5 x 60 minutes
    210 minutes
    Convert minutes to seconds (60 seconds in every minute)
    210 x 60 = 12600 seconds
  2. Convert the distance to metres
    1000 metres in every kilometre
    200 x 1000 = 200000m
  3. velocity = distance/time
    200000/12600 = 15.87m/s
    Rounded to whole numbers, as supplied in the question
    velocity = 16m/s
