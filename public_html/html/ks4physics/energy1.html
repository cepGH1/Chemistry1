Specific Heat Capacity

The amount of energy required to make a substances temperature change varies from substance to substance depending on that substances specific heat capacity.
Measured in Joules/kilogram/oC.
Calculation Example

Latent Heat

The energy per kilogram needed to get a substance to change state
This takes place at constant temperature
Example.
Boiling potatoes. The spuds are cooked at a constant 100oC because the temperature of the boiling water stays constant because the water is changing state to become steam.
