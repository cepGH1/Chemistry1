Speed

Speed = Distance/Time
Speed = metres/seconds
How far something travels in a certain amount of time

Example

If a runner runs 100 metres in 10 seconds they are travelling at 10m/s [10ms-1]
Their speed = 100/10 m/s = 10m/s

Speed Calculations

  1. Trev drives 25 km to work.The journey takes 20 minutes.
    What is Trev’s average speed in metres per second?
  2. A rocket is traveling at 20 m/s. How far does it travel in 3 hours?
  3. Steve travels 150 km in 3 hours.
    James travels 110 km in 2 hours.
    Who is traveling faster?
  4. In a 1500m race, Sam completes each 400 metre lap in 80 seconds.
    Calculate Sam’s average speed in metres per second.
  5. How long would it take Sam to run 10km at the speed from the previous question?
