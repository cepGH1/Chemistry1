Respiration

Using glucose to get energy.

Aerobic Respiration

Animals and plants and bacteria all use the energy given out by the reaction of glucose and oxygen. This process is called aerobic respiration. Or just respiration.

glucose + oxygen --> water + carbon dioxide

C6H12O6 + 6O2 --> 6H2O + 6CO2

Anaerobic Respiration

When no oxygen is available many creatures can still extract energy from glucose using other chemical reactions.

Fermentation
