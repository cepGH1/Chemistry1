Photosynthesis

The process of making glucose(a simple type of sugar) and oxygen from a mixture carbon dioxide and water.
Green plants use this process to capture the energy from sunlight.
The photosynthesis reaction is an endothermic reaction

Word Equation

Carbon dioxide + water --> glucose + water

Formula Equation

6CO2 + 6H2O --> C6H12O6 + 6O2

Why?

The plants use the glucose to give themselves the energy they need live and grow.
Photosynthesis happens in organelles called chloroplasts inside the plant's cells.
A substance called chlorophyll does photosynthesis
Plants that photosynthesise appear green because that is the part of the spectrum of light that the chloroplasts can't absorb.
