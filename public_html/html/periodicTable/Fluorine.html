Fluorine

Properties

Pale yellow poisonous gas.
Very reactive. The most reactive non metal.
  • Atomic Number : 9
  • Atomic Mass : 18.9984
  • Melting Point : -219.62 oC
  • Boiling Point : -188.1 oC

Bonding


Fluorine is a covalent diatomic molecule
Fluorine makes covalent bonds to other non-metals
Fluorine makes fluoride ions F- when reacted with metals
2Na(s) + F2(g) --> 2NaF(s)

Reactions

Fluorine gas reacts violently with most other elements
  • F2 + H2 --> 2HF
    With Hydrogen gas it makes Hydrogen Fluoride
    Periodic Table of videos / Fluorine
  • With alkali metals
    For example, Caesium
    2Cs + F2 --> 2CsF
