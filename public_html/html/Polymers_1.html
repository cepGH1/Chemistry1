Polymers

Polymers are molecules made up of repeating units called monomers.
A simple model for a polymer is a link of beads. The necklace( the polymer) is made up of many beads (the monomers)
On a chemical scale the polymer is a large molecule and is made up of many repeats of small structures.

Examples of polymers

Plastic polythene
DNA
Protein
