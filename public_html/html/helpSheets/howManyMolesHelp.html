How to calculate the number of Moles in a substance

The formula to do this is

To work out how many moles you have of a substance, divide the mass of the substance by the relative mass of the substance. Either the relative atomic mass for an element that is made up of single atoms, or the relative formula mass for any molecular element or compound.

Examples

1. Calculate how many moles of potassium there are in 90g of potassium metal.

  • moles = mass/relative mass
  • mass = 90g
  • relative mass : in this case the relative atomic mass of potassium K = 39.098
  • moles = 90/39.098 = 2.302mol

2. Calculate how many moles of Magnesium there are in 300g of Magnesium metal.

  • moles = mass/relative mass
  • mass = 300g
  • relative mass : in this case the relative atomic mass of Magnesium Mg = 24.305
  • moles = 300/24.305 = 12.343mol

3. Calculate how many moles of Fluorine there are in 50g of Fluorine gas.

  • moles = mass/relative mass
  • mass = 50g
  • relative mass : in this case the relative molecular mass of Fluorine F2 = 18.998 + 18.998 = 37.996
    Note that Fluorine exists as a diatomic molecule and we need to use it's relative formula mass in this calculation.
  • moles = 50/37.996 = 1.316mol

4. Calculate how many moles of Sodium Chloride there are in 200g of Sodium Chloride crystals.

  • moles = mass/relative mass
  • mass = 200g
  • relative mass : in this case the relative formula mass of Sodium Chloride NaCl = 22.990 + 35.45 = 58.44
  • moles = 200/58.44 = 3.422mol

5. Calculate how many moles of Potassium Hydroxide there are in 89g of Potassium Hydroxide pellets.

  • moles = mass/relative mass
  • mass = 89g
  • relative mass : in this case the relative formula mass of Potassium Hydroxide KOH = 39.098 + 15.999 + 1.008 = 56.105
  • moles = 89/56.105 = 1.586mol

6. Calculate how many moles of Ammonium Phosphate there are in 500g of Ammonium Phosphate.

  • moles = mass/relative mass
  • mass = 500g
  • relative mass : in this case the relative formula mass of Ammonium Phosphate (NH4)3PO4
    = (14.007 + (1.008 x 4)) x 3) + 30.974 + (4 x 15.999) = 149.087
  • moles = 500/149.087 = 3.354mol
