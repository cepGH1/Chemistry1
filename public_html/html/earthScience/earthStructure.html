Structure of Planet Earth

The Earth has four layers.
  1. Crust - the outer layer we see
  2. Mantle - hot rock layer below the crust, kept solid by pressure
  3. Outer core - liquid metal layer
  4. Inner core - solid metal inner layer

Chemical Composition of the Earth layers

  1. Crust : Complex mixture of inorganic and organic compounds
  2. Mantel : Silicate rocks (see silicon dioxide)
  3. Outer Core : Iron (Fe) 80%, Nickel (Ni) 5%, other metals 15%
    The NiFe alloy
  4. Inner Core : Iron (Fe)
